Canonical span codes. One lifecycle marker plus per-pattern codes that match each pattern’s otel_span in crate::patterns::PATTERN_REGISTRY.

All constants are u16 raw values; wrap them in SpanCode when you need the typed wrapper: SpanCode(span::TICK).
